Emergency Contacts

Quick access to emergency services in critical situations.

Why Are Emergency Contacts Important?

During medical emergencies, accidents, or natural disasters, knowing whom to call can be **life-saving**. Always keep these emergency numbers handy and accessible.

üìû Universal Emergency Numbers

  • **Emergency Services (General):** 112 (International Standard)
  • **Ambulance:** 911 (USA), 108 (India), 999 (UK)
  • **Fire Department:** 101
  • **Police:** 100
  • **Poison Control:** +1-800-222-1222 (USA), 102 (India)

üè• Medical Emergency Contacts

  • **Local Hospitals:** Contact your nearest hospital for quick assistance.
  • **Red Cross Helpline:** +1-800-733-2767 (USA)
  • **Cardiac Emergency Hotline:** Check for your country-specific numbers.

üöë What to Say When Calling for Help?

When calling an emergency number, provide the following details:

  • Your **exact location** (address or landmarks).
  • The **nature of the emergency** (accident, medical, fire, etc.).
  • Number of **people involved**.
  • Your **contact details**.
  • Follow the instructions given by the emergency operator.
