Steps for Helping Someone with a Heart Attack

  1. Call **emergency services immediately**.
  2. Keep the person **calm and seated**.
  3. Give **aspirin** (unless allergic).
  4. If they collapse, perform **CPR**.
  5. Continue until medical help arrives.
