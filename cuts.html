Steps for Treating Fractures

  1. Keep the injured area **immobilized**.
  2. Apply a **splint** to stabilize the broken bone.
  3. Use an ice pack to **reduce swelling**.
  4. Do not attempt to realign the bone.
  5. Seek immediate medical help.
