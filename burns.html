Steps for Treating Burns

  1. Cool the burn with cold running water for at least 10 minutes.
  2. Remove tight clothing or jewelry near the burn area.
  3. Cover the burn with a clean, non-stick bandage.
  4. Do not apply butter, ice, or ointments.
  5. Seek medical help if the burn is severe.
